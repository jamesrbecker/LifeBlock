# LifeBlocks Social Media Marketing Strategy

## Brand Voice & Personality

**Tone:** Motivational but relatable. Self-aware. Occasionally funny. Never preachy.

**Character:** The app's social accounts are like a supportive friend who:
- Gets the struggle of building habits
- Celebrates small wins
- Makes productivity feel less intimidating
- Uses humor to connect (not corporate cringe)

### Instagram - @lifeblocks

**Content Mix:**
1. **Reels (50%)** - Quick tips, transformations, "watch my grid fill up"
2. **Stories (30%)** - Daily check-in prompts, polls, user shoutouts
3. **Static Posts (20%)** - Motivational graphics, milestone cards, path features

**Reel Ideas:**
- "Day 1 vs Day 30 of tracking habits" (grid filling up timelapse)
- "Pick your life path" (showing all 16 options)
- "POV: The app that finally made habits stick"
- "How I went from 0 to 100 day streak"
- "What habit trackers get wrong" (talking head)

**Story Strategy:**
- Daily: "Did you check in today?" poll
- Weekly: User milestone celebrations
- Ask questions: "What's your biggest habit struggle?"

## Growth Tactics

### 1. User-Generated Content
- Encourage users to share their milestone cards
- Repost streak celebrations
- Create a branded hashtag challenge

### 2. Influencer Partnerships
- Micro-influencers (5-50K followers) in:
  - Productivity/self-improvement
  - Fitness/health
  - Students/study accounts
  - Entrepreneurs/side hustle
- Offer free Premium in exchange for honest review

### 3. Community Building
- Create a Discord or community for users
- Weekly challenges ("7-day streak challenge")
- Leaderboards (opt-in)

### 4. Content Pillars (Weekly Schedule)

| Day | Content Type | Example |
|-----|--------------|---------|
| Monday | Motivational | "Your week starts now. One check-in at a time." |
| Tuesday | Tips/How-to | "3 habits every entrepreneur should track" |
| Wednesday | Relatable/Funny | Meme about habit struggles |
| Thursday | Feature highlight | "Did you know you can track 3 paths?" |
| Friday | Celebration | User milestone shoutout |
| Saturday | Weekend energy | "Rest is productive too" |
| Sunday | Reflection | "How was your week? Rate your grid 1-10" |

## Reply Guy Playbook

**Accounts to Follow & Engage:**
- @JamesClear (Atomic Habits author)
- @hubaborian (habit content)
- Fitness influencers posting transformation content
- Entrepreneurs sharing daily routines
- Students posting study content
- Creators sharing their creative process

**Types of Replies:**

1. **Supportive** - Celebrate their wins genuinely
2. **Additive** - Add value to their point
3. **Witty** - Clever observation that makes people click your profile
4. **Relatable** - "This is so real" energy

**What NOT to do:**
- Don't spam "download my app"
- Don't be negative or argumentative
- Don't reply to everything (be selective)
- Don't fake engagement

**When to Plug:**
- Only when genuinely relevant
- "This is exactly why I built LifeBlocks" (once per week max)
- In your bio: "Building @lifeblocks ðŸŸ©"

## Launch Campaign Ideas

### "30 Day Challenge"
- Challenge users to track for 30 days
- Daily prompts and check-ins on social
- Prize: Free lifetime Premium for participants who complete it
- Share milestone cards throughout

### "What's Your Path?"
- Quiz-style content: "Are you an Entrepreneur or Creator?"
- User picks their path and shares
- Drives curiosity about the 16 paths

### "Build In Public"
- Share development updates
- User counts, features added
- Creates authenticity and following

## Metrics to Track

- App Store clicks from social
- Profile visits
- Mentions and shares
