Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Summary: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, sell, or share your personal information.

What Data We Collect

We collect nothing. All your habit tracking data, check-ins, and personal information are stored locally on your device and optionally synced via your personal iCloud account.

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All app data is stored on your device using Apple's secure storage systems.
  • iCloud Sync: If you enable iCloud, your data syncs across your devices through your personal Apple account. We never have access to this data.
  • HealthKit: If you grant permission, we read exercise and sleep data from Apple Health to auto-fill your check-ins. This data is never transmitted off your device.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store: Handles all payment processing for subscriptions. We don't see your payment information.
  • Apple Push Notifications: Used for daily reminders. Notification tokens are managed by Apple.

Analytics

We use Apple's built-in App Analytics to see aggregate, anonymized data like download counts and crash reports. This contains no personally identifiable information.

Data Sharing

We never sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app.
  • Export: Premium users can export all their data as JSON.
  • Deletion: Delete the app to remove all local data. iCloud data can be deleted via iOS Settings.

Children's Privacy

LifeGrid does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences focused on personal development.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated through app updates.

Contact
