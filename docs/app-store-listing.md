## App Store Description

**Transform your daily habits into a visual journey of progress.**

LifeBlocks helps you build the life you want—one block at a time. Watch your consistency grow with a beautiful GitHub-style contribution grid that fills in as you complete your daily habits.

**CHOOSE YOUR PATH**
Whether you're a content creator, entrepreneur, student, nurse, electrician, or parent—LifeBlocks has curated habits designed for your journey. Pick from 35+ life paths or create your own.

**VISUAL PROGRESS**
See your dedication come to life with a stunning activity grid. Each day you show up, another block fills in. Watch your streaks grow and celebrate milestones at 7, 21, 30, and 100+ days.

**FEATURES**
• GitHub-style contribution grid shows your consistency
• 35+ life paths with curated habits for every profession
• Custom habit creation with icons and colors
• Streak tracking with milestone celebrations
• Home screen widgets to stay motivated
• Beautiful dark theme design
• iCloud sync across all your devices
• Siri shortcuts for quick check-ins

**WHY LIFEBLOCKS?**
Most habit apps focus on streaks alone. LifeBlocks shows you the bigger picture—your entire journey visualized. One look at your grid tells the story of your dedication.

**PATHS FOR EVERYONE**
- Content Creators & Influencers
- Entrepreneurs & Founders
- Software Engineers & Developers
- Healthcare: Nurses, EMTs, Doctors
- Trades: Electricians, Plumbers, Welders, Mechanics
- Students & Teachers
- Athletes & Fitness Enthusiasts
- Parents & Caregivers
- And 25+ more paths...

**PREMIUM FEATURES**
Unlock unlimited habits, custom paths, advanced analytics, and more with LifeBlocks Pro.

Start building your best life today. Your future self will thank you.
