# Privacy Policy for LifeBlocks

**Last Updated: January 23, 2026**

## Introduction

LifeBlocks ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Your name (optional, for personalization only)
- **Habit Data**: The habits you create, track, and complete
- **Preferences**: Your selected life path, theme preferences, and app settings

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app (features used, check-in times)
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, for app optimization

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do **NOT** collect your email address (unless you contact support)
- We do **NOT** collect your location data
- We do **NOT** collect contacts or photos
- We do **NOT** sell your data to third parties

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the LifeBlocks service
- Track your habits and streaks locally on your device
- Sync your data across your devices (if you enable iCloud)
- Send optional notifications and reminders (with your permission)
- Improve our app based on anonymized usage patterns

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
Your habit data is primarily stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This means your data stays on your device and is protected by your device's security features.

### iCloud Sync (Optional)
If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account. This data is encrypted and protected by Apple's security measures. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

## Third-Party Services

### RevenueCat
We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect:
- Anonymous purchase data
- Subscription status

RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### Apple Services
- **App Store**: Purchase and subscription processing
- **iCloud**: Optional data sync (if enabled)
- **StoreKit**: In-app purchase verification

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your data within the app
- **Delete**: Delete your account and all associated data at any time through Settings > Delete Account
- **Export**: Your data remains on your device and in your iCloud account

## Data Retention

- Local data is retained until you delete the app or clear app data
- iCloud data is retained until you delete it from your iCloud account
- We do not retain any personal data on our servers

## Children's Privacy

LifeBlocks is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app can be used without providing any personal information.

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- All data is stored locally on your device
- iCloud data is encrypted by Apple
- No sensitive data is transmitted to our servers

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

**Email**: support@lifeblocks.app
