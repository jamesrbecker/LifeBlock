## App Store Description (Full)

**LifeBlocks - Visual Habit Tracker**

*Stack your days. Build your life.*

LifeBlocks transforms habit tracking into a visual journey. Inspired by GitHub's contribution graph, watch your consistency grow as you build colorful streaks of progress.

**THE PHILOSOPHY**

Life is about building blocks. Every skill learned, every habit formed, every day of progress adds another block to your foundation. LifeBlocks makes this philosophy visual - see your commitment grow one square at a time.

**CORE FEATURES**

Visual Progress Grid
- GitHub-style contribution graph for your life
- See patterns in your habits at a glance
- Watch your consistency grow over time
- Multiple color themes to match your style

Smart Daily Check-ins
- Quick 30-second daily reviews
- Track multiple habits per day
- Add notes and reflections
- HealthKit integration for automatic tracking

Streak Tracking
- Overall streak counter with milestones
- Per-habit individual streaks
- Shareable streak cards for social media
- Motivational milestone celebrations

**WIDGETS**

- Home screen widgets (small, medium, large)
- Lock screen widgets for quick glances
- StandBy mode support for iOS 17
- Interactive widgets to check in from anywhere

**SOCIAL FEATURES (NEW in 2.0)**

- Connect with friends via unique codes
- Send cheers to celebrate achievements
- Accountability partner mode
- View friends' progress for motivation

**MULTI-PLATFORM**

- iPhone: Optimized for all screen sizes
- iPad: Beautiful split-view layout
- Mac: Native Catalyst support with keyboard shortcuts
- Apple Watch: Coming soon

**PREMIUM FEATURES**

Unlock the full potential with LifeBlocks Premium:
- Unlimited habits (free tier: 5 habits)
- All color themes and customization
- Weekly and monthly progress reports
- Friends and accountability features
- Priority support

**PRIVACY FOCUSED**

Your data stays on your device and syncs via iCloud. We don't have access to your habits or personal information.
