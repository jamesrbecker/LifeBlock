## App Description

### Short Description (170 characters)
Transform your daily habits into a beautiful visual journey. Track progress, compete in challenges, and build the life you've always wanted with LifeBlocks.

### Full Description

**Build the life you deserve, one block at a time.**

LifeBlocks transforms your daily habits into a beautiful GitHub-style contribution grid. Watch your consistency grow as each day becomes a colorful block in your life's masterpiece.

**WHY LIFEBLOCKS?**

Unlike other habit trackers that overwhelm you with data, LifeBlocks uses a simple visual system. Green blocks = progress. The more consistent you are, the more vibrant your grid becomes.

**FEATURES THAT WORK:**

Visual Progress Tracking
- GitHub-style contribution grid shows your year at a glance
- Color intensity reflects your daily commitment
- Watch patterns emerge as you build momentum

Choose Your Path
- Fitness Warrior - Build strength and endurance
- Creative Mind - Nurture your artistic side
- Mindful Soul - Cultivate inner peace
- Knowledge Seeker - Expand your learning
- Health Champion - Optimize your wellbeing
- And many more paths to choose from

Smart Notifications
- Gentle daily reminders
- Streak protection alerts
- Weekly progress summaries

Challenges & Competition
- Join global challenges
- Compete with friends
- Earn achievements

Widgets & Lock Screen
- See your streak at a glance
- Quick check-in from home screen
- Lock screen widgets for iOS 17+

**PREMIUM FEATURES:**

- Unlimited habits
- Advanced analytics
- Multiple life paths
- iCloud sync
- Priority support

**JOIN THOUSANDS building better lives, one day at a time.**

### Competitor Differentiation:
- Visual (GitHub-style grid) vs. list-based trackers
- Life paths vs. generic habit templates
- Social challenges vs. solo tracking
- Beautiful design vs. utility-first apps

### Seasonal Updates:
- January: New Year resolutions focus
- September: Back to school/routine reset
- Summer: Fitness challenges
- Holiday: Gratitude and wellness focus

## Review Request Strategy

Trigger review prompt when:
1. User completes 7-day streak (first milestone)
2. User completes a challenge
3. User shares their progress card
4. After 14 days of active use

Never show:
- During onboarding
- After a missed day
- More than once per 30 days
