## DESCRIPTION (4000 characters max)

```
Transform your daily habits into a beautiful visual journey. LifeBlocks helps you build consistency, track progress, and achieve your goals with an intuitive grid-based habit tracker.

**YOUR LIFE, ONE BLOCK AT A TIME**

Every day you complete your habits, you fill in a block on your personal grid. Watch your consistency grow as patterns emerge, streaks build, and your progress becomes visible at a glance.

**CHOOSE YOUR LIFE PATH**

Select from 38+ career-focused paths including:
• Tech: Software Engineer, Game Developer
• Creative: Content Creator, Musician, Writer, Artist
• Business: Entrepreneur, Investor, Sales, Real Estate
• Healthcare: Doctor, Nurse, EMT, Physical Therapist
• Trades: Electrician, Plumber, Welder, Carpenter, Mechanic
• Lifestyle: Student, Athlete, Parent, Digital Nomad
• And many more...

Each path comes with curated habit suggestions, motivational quotes, and a personalized experience designed for your journey.

**KEY FEATURES**

• Visual Grid Tracker - See your entire year at a glance with a beautiful color-coded grid
• Daily Check-ins - Quick and satisfying way to mark your habits complete
• Streak Tracking - Build momentum with consecutive day streaks
• Smart Reminders - Customizable notifications to keep you on track
• HealthKit Integration - Auto-track exercise and sleep from Apple Health
• Home Screen Widgets - Multiple sizes to keep your progress visible
• Dark & Light Mode - Beautiful design that adapts to your preference

**SOCIAL & COMPETITIVE**

• Global Leaderboards - Compete with users worldwide
• Path Leaderboards - See how you rank among others on your same path
• Friends System - Add friends and cheer each other on
• Challenges - Join timed challenges to push your limits

**PREMIUM FEATURES**

Upgrade to unlock your full potential:
• Unlimited custom habits (free users get 3)
• All widget sizes for your home screen
• Leaderboard access
• Challenge participation
• Premium badge and priority support

**PRIVACY FIRST**

Your habit data stays on your device. We don't sell your personal information. Leaderboard participation is optional and opt-in only.

**FLEXIBLE PLANS**

• Free tier with core features
• Monthly and yearly subscriptions
• Family plans for up to 5 members
• Lifetime purchase option

Start building better habits today. One block at a time.
```

## REVIEW NOTES FOR APPLE

```
LifeBlocks is a habit tracking app that helps users build daily routines.

**Demo Account:** Not required - the app works without login.

**In-App Purchases:**
- Monthly subscription: $2.99/month
- Yearly subscription: $29.99/year
- Lifetime: $79.99
- Family plans also available

**HealthKit Usage:**
The app optionally reads exercise minutes and sleep data from HealthKit to auto-track related habits. This is opt-in and data never leaves the device.

**Notifications:**
Used for daily habit reminders. Fully customizable by the user.

**CloudKit:**
Used only for optional leaderboard functionality when users opt-in.
```
